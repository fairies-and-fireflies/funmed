Pediatric and Adult Functional Medicine Membership/Consult

Who is this plan for?  

This plan is the best choice for anyone with a chronic health condition or other wellness goals such as weight loss, gut concerns, skin conditions, hormone imbalances, or general concerns such as fatigue, etc. 

Cost: 
  • $450 initial visit fee
  • $150/month (6 month minimum, 12 month commitment recommended)

Monthly membership includes once a month 60 minute provider follow up visits and communication/support as needed throughout the month.  If you are interested in a one time consult visit instead of membership, the hourly rate will apply.  Hourly rates available for $300/hour.

Menopause Wellness Plan 

Who is this plan for?

This plan is for any woman 35 years or older who is interested in optimizing health throughout menopause.  

Cost:
  • $325 initial visit fee
  • $25/month (12 month commitment recommended) 

Includes continued management and refills for bio identical hormone replacement therapy (BHRT), an annual 60 minute visit with lab follow up, and periodic 30 minute check in appointments as needed.  Additionally, access and recommendations for vetted supplements and labs may be included in your personalized plan of care for hormone health.

Annual Wellness Plan 

Who is this plan for?

This plan is perfect for just about anyone who is wanting to approach their health and wellness journey from a functional lens.  This is also a wonderful option for those who have graduated our functional medicine membership or menopause wellness plan and are wanting yearly health maintenance. 

Cost:
  • $300/year annual fee

Includes yearly lab work recommendations and analysis, personalized nutrition and supplements, and personalized lifestyle plan for optimum health.  Includes a 60 minute annual appointment and a 60 minute lab follow up.
