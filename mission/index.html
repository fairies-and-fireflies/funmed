What is Functional Medicine?

 

Functional medicine strives to address health concerns through a holistic, root cause lens.  Shifting from the traditional disease-centered focus to a personalized, patient-centered approach, functional medicine addresses the whole person, not an isolated set of symptoms.   At its core, functional medicine strives to help the bodily systems of each individual operate to the best of its ability. 

This is accomplished by spending time with patients, listening to their stories, and looking at interactions among genetic, environmental, and lifestyle factors that can influence long term health and wellness.  In this way, each individual is able to optimize their own health and vitality. 

At Renaissance Health Group, we ask how and why illness occurs.  Chronic disease is usually preceded by a period of declining function in one or more of the body’s systems. Identifying and optimizing each dysfunction as early as possible allows for improvement or prevention of chronic disease and the optimization of wellness.
