Functional Medicine Membership

Who is this plan for?  

This plan is the best choice for anyone with a chronic health condition or other wellness goals such as weight loss, gut concerns, skin conditions, hormone imbalances, or general concerns such as fatigue, etc. 

Cost: 
  • $400 initial visit fee with one of our providers
  • $250/month (6 month minimum, 12 month commitment recommended)

Monthly membership includes monthly health coaching, functional nutrition services, access to our vetted supplements, and continued provider follow up.

Health Coaching

Who is this plan for? 

This plan is wonderful for anyone needing support in reaching their health goals.  Areas that health coaching may address include: 

  • Habit formation around all areas of wellness, i.e., eating, weight, exercise, stress reduction, relationships, & your spiritual life
  • Creating better balance with work, family, & leisure time
  • Creating goals, dreams, and future plans for all areas of living, i.e., love, family, recreation, leisure, spiritual, work
  • Time management & organization
  • Maintaining fitness & initiating exercise
Cost:
  • $50 per visit

This service may be added to any of the other service options or used by itself to reach your goals.  This service is included in a functional medicine care plan.

Functional Nutrition

Who is this plan for?

This is a great service for anyone needing more targeted nutrition support.  This could include those with a history of restricted eating, children who are picky eaters, or anyone who wants to work with a registered functional dietician.  

Cost:
  • $100 per visit

This service may be added to any of the other service options or used by itself to reach your goals.  This service is included in a functional medicine care plan.

Menopause Wellness Plan 

Who is this plan for?

This plan is for any woman 35 years or older who is interested in optimizing health throughout menopause.  

Cost:
  • $400 initial visit fee with one of our providers 
  • $300/year annual fee

Includes continued management and refills for BHRT, a yearly visit check in with one of our providers, access and recommendations for vetted supplements, yearly lab work recommendations and personalized plan of care for hormone health.

Annual Wellness Plan 

Who is this plan for?

This plan is perfect for just about anyone who is wanting to approach their health and wellness journey from a functional lens.  This is also a wonderful option for those who have graduated our functional medicine membership plan and are wanting yearly health maintenance. 

Cost:
  • $400 initial visit fee with one of our providers
  • $300/year annual fee

Includes lab work recommendations and analysis, personalized nutrition and supplements, and personalized lifestyle plan for optimum health.
